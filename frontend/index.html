About ShipShare Kenya

Making shipping affordable for every Kenyan through smart group logistics

Our Mission

ShipShare Kenya was founded with a simple mission: to make shipping affordable and accessible for every Kenyan. We noticed that many individuals and small businesses were struggling with high shipping costs, especially when sending packages to different parts of the country.

By connecting people who are shipping to similar destinations, we help everyone save money while reducing the environmental impact of multiple separate deliveries.

Our Story

Founded in 2023, ShipShare Kenya started as a small solution for Nairobi-based online sellers who wanted to reduce their shipping costs. Today, we've grown to serve thousands of customers across Kenya, from individuals sending care packages to small businesses managing their logistics.

Our platform has helped Kenyans save over KES 2.5 million in shipping costs while building a community of smart shippers who understand the power of collaboration.

Our Values

Collaboration

We believe in the power of working together to achieve common goals and reduce costs for everyone.

Sustainability

By consolidating shipments, we help reduce the carbon footprint of logistics in Kenya.

Trust

We ensure every shipment is secure and every user can trust our platform with their packages.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much can I really save?

Most users save between 40-70% on shipping costs by joining groups. The more people in your group, the more everyone saves!

Is my package safe?

Yes! All shipments are insured up to KES 50,000 and tracked in real-time. We also verify all users in groups.

How long does shipping take?

Delivery times vary by route: Nairobi-Mombasa (2-3 days), Nairobi-Kisumu (1-2 days), other routes (1-4 days).
