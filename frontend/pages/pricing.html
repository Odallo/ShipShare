Pricing Questions

How is pricing calculated?

Pricing is based on route distance, package weight, and group size. The more people in a group, the less everyone pays!

Are there any hidden fees?

No! The price you see is the price you pay. No booking fees, no service charges, no surprises.

Can I cancel my shipment?

Yes, you can cancel up to 2 hours before pickup for a full refund. After that, a 20% cancellation fee applies.

Do you offer volume discounts?

Yes! Business and Enterprise plans offer significant volume discounts. Contact our sales team for custom pricing.
