About ShipShare Kenya

Revolutionizing shipping costs across Kenya through collaborative logistics

Our Mission

To make shipping affordable and accessible for all Kenyans by connecting people who need to ship items with others traveling to the same destination, reducing costs through shared logistics.

We believe that everyone deserves access to affordable shipping services, whether you're a small business owner, student, or individual sending packages to loved ones.

Our Vision

To become Kenya's leading collaborative shipping platform, saving millions of shillings for Kenyans while reducing our carbon footprint through optimized logistics.
