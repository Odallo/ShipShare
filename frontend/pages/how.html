Detailed Process

Step 1: Create Your Account

Sign up with your email and create a password. Add your phone number for easy communication. Your account is created instantly and you can start browsing groups immediately.

Step 2: Find or Create a Group

Use our smart filters to find groups going to your destination. Filter by origin, destination, package type, and group status. If no suitable group exists, create your own and let others join you.

Step 3: Prepare Your Shipment

Enter your package details including weight, dimensions, and contents. Our system calculates your cost and shows you exactly how much you'll save compared to individual shipping.

Step 4: Secure Payment

Pay securely using M-Pesa, bank transfer, or credit card. Your payment is held in escrow until delivery is confirmed, ensuring your money is always safe.

Step 5: Package Pickup

Our courier partner picks up your package from your location. All packages are insured up to KES 50,000 for complete peace of mind.

Step 6: Real-time Tracking

Track your shipment in real-time through our app. Get notifications when your package is picked up, in transit, and delivered. Know exactly when to expect your delivery.

Step 7: Delivery Confirmation

Once your package is delivered, confirm receipt and rate your experience. Your payment is released to the courier, and you can see your total savings in your dashboard.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much can I save?

Most users save between 40-70% compared to individual shipping costs. The exact amount depends on your route and group size.

Is my package insured?

Yes! All shipments are insured up to KES 50,000 at no additional cost for complete peace of mind.

How long does shipping take?

Delivery times vary by route: Nairobi-Mombasa (2-3 days), Nairobi-Kisumu (1-2 days), other routes (1-4 days).

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept M-Pesa, bank transfers, and all major credit/debit cards for your convenience.
